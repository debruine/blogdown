Biography

Lisa DeBruine is a professor of psychology at the University of Glasgow School of Psychology and Neuroscience. She is a founding member and current Associate Director of the Psychological Science Accelerator, a member of the APA Open Science and Methodology Committee, a local lead for the UK Reproducibility Network, a member of the ORCiD Researcher Advisory Council, and a member of the psyTeachR team for reproducible research educational resources.
